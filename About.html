Aug 13 2019,

Both of us are just newbies, still studying IT Programming at LITHAN SCHOOL. How we found is after the module of website production, we noticed that two of us are passionated in creating the webpages, fascinated in designing for the different and epic functions.Among them, our main target is to build incredible websites that are distinct from others, as well as to help people and make our country one of those.

After a few days we have been created,

Moments That Made Us Hard

Creating a team with two newbies is not that much easy. There are certain challenges and dangers. Especially with our friends, several of our friends have suffered us from being split because we did not call them up in this team. However, there is nothing we could do about it. As a consequences at that time, we two always felt terrible and also tried to split up the team. But those times were just for a while and later they were gone as both of us are totally passionated about the project. It's like no matter how complicated the situation is, we two will keep on trying because we both have our own ambitions and priorities.
